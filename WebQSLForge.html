📜 WebQSLForge User Manual

ADIF to manager, the easy way

The intention of this program is to provide a simple method for creating QSL cards directly from an ADIF file. The program requires an internet connection to sync prefixes. Since it's a standalone HTML file, it works seamlessly on Windows, Mac, and Linux, as well as on your personal or club website. This tool was made with love by an amateur for amateurs, with simplicity and intuitiveness in focus. By utilizing the ADIF 3.1.6 standard, it bridges the gap between digital logging and physical card exchange so you can make QSL card the easy way With this program you can make the cards and print them and it will be sorted for you to.

So how do you use it? That is very Easy... Start by uploading a background image for your card and an ADIF file containing your QSOs using the menu on the left. Once loaded, all contacts will appear in the table below, and you can click any row to preview that specific QSL card. Customize your card by entering your station details, choosing a font, and adjusting colors, transparency, and borders. You can drag text elements directly on the preview to position them, and use the scroll wheel to resize them. The background image can also be zoomed, moved, and rotated with your mouse. When everything looks right, export your QSL cards to a PDF with a single click. The cards are automatically sorted with the power and the automatic collaboration of QSL BY BUREAU before you printing it out

Are we GDPR Compliant? YES... WebQSLForge is designed with privacy in mind and is, by default, GDPR compliant. The application runs locally in your browser and does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers. All ADIF log data and user inputs remain on your own device. The only external requests made by the application are for publicly available prefix data (used for sorting and country identification) and for loading fonts from Google Fonts to ensure proper visual styling. PDF generation is handled locally within your browser and does not involve any data being sent externally. No personal or sensitive information is intentionally shared, stored, or processed outside of your local environment. No Data Processing Agreement is needed on our end.

🖱️ Mouse & Interaction Guide

On Text Elements:

  • Move: Left-click and hold to drag any text box to a new position.
  • Resize: Hover over an element and use the Scroll Wheel to change font size.

On Background Image:

  • Zoom: Use the Scroll Wheel on an empty area to zoom the image.
  • Pan: Click and hold the Right Mouse Button to slide the image.
  • Rotate: Click and hold the Middle Mouse Button to rotate the image.

Core Features:

  • Dynamic Filtering: Filter by date, time, and specific countries.
  • Interactive Design: Drag text to move, use mouse wheel to scale.
  • Auto-Sort: PDF export is automatically sorted by country for easy Bureau handling.
